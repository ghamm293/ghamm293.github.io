g. c. HammondMixed Media Artist


I am a lover of the arts who aims to evoke a mixture of awe and wonder through my creations. I believe that art should display aspects of mystery. Emotions portrayed by the human face are by far my favourite vehicle of mystery. The eyes are said to be the windows of the soul and thus I believe we should fill them with a world that is beyond what is before us. Others should see the beauty of the face in each fractal aspect it displays. Whether it is sculpture, drawing, or watercolour I hope to "wash away the dust of everyday life" through my artwork as Pablo Picasso once said.
